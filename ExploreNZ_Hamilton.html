The City of Hamilton

Situated in the central North Island, the diverse Waikato region is well known for its nature-based tourism, Lord of the Rings and Middle-earth scenic expeditions and the iconic Waikato River. The region offers a wide range for all types of visitors and tourists, from Hamilton city’s attractions to the amazing sandy surf beaches of Raglan and the rolling hills and mountains that define the landscape.

Hamilton is New Zealand’s fourth largest city, a large metropolitan hub on the banks of the Waikato River. With great shopping, a colourful arts scene, a fine range of dining and a pumping night scene, Hamilton has urban entertainment to suit all interests and ages. Exploration is easy and enjoyable across Hamilton and its surrounds – gardens, zoos, hotels and casinos, museums, food, walks and scenery everywhere make it a must-see place for any visitor.

Classic cars, river cruises, microbrewery tours and more are on offer, and for the pinnacle of views of the city, a hot air balloon ride is a big, big must. The city is host to a number of international events such as the annual Balloons Over Waikato, and attracts many people from the wider region, as well as Auckland from its business and commercial setting.
